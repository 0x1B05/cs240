\problem{}
Before the beginning of a new semester at ShanghaiTech University, there are \(n\) students who need to register for courses and \(m\) available courses. The registration process is subject to the following constraints:

1. Eligibility constraint: Each student may only register for courses that are allowed under their major requirements.

2. Student course limit: Each student \(i\) may enroll in at most \(k_i\) courses during the semester.

3. Course capacity limit: Each course \(j\) has a capacity limit and can admit at most \(c_j\) students.

Your task is to formulate this problem as a network flow model and explain the maximum number of valid student-course assignments subject to the constraints above.
